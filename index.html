Chrome Extension

A Chrome extension that detects bot-like behavior across Twitter, Telegram, and Google Meet by analyzing account activity, response timing, and linguistic patterns.

  • Heuristic + pattern-based analysis to assign a human authenticity score in real time
  • Lightweight overlay UI that injects seamlessly on supported platforms without disrupting experience
